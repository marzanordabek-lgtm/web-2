Makoto Shinkai

Born 1973

About Makoto Shinkai

Makoto Shinkai is a Japanese filmmaker, animator, and manga artist. He is best known for creating visually stunning and emotionally powerful animated films, including Your Name and Weathering With You. His works are celebrated worldwide for their beautiful art and storytelling.

Major Life Events

  1. 1973 - Born in Nagano, Japan
  2. 1999 - Released short film "She and Her Cat"
  3. 2002 - Released "Voices of a Distant Star"
  4. 2016 - Released hit film "Your Name"
  5. 2019 - Released "Weathering With You"

Key Contributions

  • Directed multiple critically acclaimed animated feature films
  • Created short films and manga works
  • Known for a distinct, beautiful visual style
  • Popularized modern anime storytelling worldwide
